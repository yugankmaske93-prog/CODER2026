Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missible Man of India | Former President of India

About

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a leading role in India's missible and nuclear weapon programs. He was widely known as the "Missible Man of India".

Achievements

  • Developed India's first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III)
  • Key role in Pokhran-II nuclear tests
  • 11th President of India (2002-2007)
  • Received Bharat Ratna in 1997

Famous Quote

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
